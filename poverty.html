Poverty And How It Connects To Human Trafficking

What is Poverty?

- The lack of resources to provide the necessities of life including food, access to transportation, clean water, clothing, and access to education.

Where in the world is poverty most located?

- According to the World Bank, poverty is most located in these places with the amount of people living in poverty:

1) 413.3 million in sub-Saharan Africa

2) 216.4 million in South Asia

3) 47.2 million in East Asia and Pacific

4) 25.9 million in Latin America and the Caribbean

5) 18.6 million in the Middle East and North Africa

6) 7.1 million in Europe and Central Asia

7) 7.3 million in the rest of the world
